Why Forensics Ties It All Together

Red team shows you how an attack happens. Blue team shows you how to catch it. Forensics shows you what actually happened — reconstructing the full timeline from artifacts that don't lie. It's the discipline that turns "we think we were breached" into "here is exactly what they did, when, and how."

Current Focus

Working through memory forensics with Volatility 3, disk artifact analysis (MFT, registry hives, prefetch), and static/dynamic malware triage. Each artifact is a witness — the goal is to make them all corroborate one timeline.
